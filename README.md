# Scope3Bridge
An ESG data on-ramp for SME suppliers 

The problem 👇
Government regulations are now putting serious pressure on large corporations to prove they are actually "Green" (Demonstrating ethical, social, and environmental responsibility while running an organization ) That means reporting emissions across their entire supply chain, This then puts pressure on SMEs (Small and Medium Enterprises).

These smaller businesses often lack the specialized resources to navigate complex carbon accounting frameworks. And without the right tools? They risk losing major clients entirely.

This is the problem Scope3Bridge is built to address, helping SMEs stay competitive by simplifying how they report sustainability data to the large corporations they work with.
